Adventure Meets Performance: Taycan 4 Cross Turismo

The Porsche Taycan 4 Cross Turismo combines electric performance with all-road capability. With standard all-wheel drive, increased ride height, and versatile cargo space, it's the perfect fusion of sportiness and practicality for modern explorers.

Powertrain

Dual electric motors (AWD)

Max power: up to 469 hp (with overboost)

Transmission

2-speed automatic on rear axle

Seats

Up to 5 seats with extended rear headroom and cargo flexibility
